TEAM LEADER

Yuan-Tang Chou 周圓唐

Yuan-Tang Chou joins NTHU as Assistant Professor of Physics in August 2026. A member of the ATLAS collaboration at CERN since 2016, his work spans exotic Higgs searches, real-time trigger systems, and AI infrastructure for science. He currently serves as ATLAS b-jet trigger signature coordinator and Fast ML foundation coprocessor subgroup coordinator.
