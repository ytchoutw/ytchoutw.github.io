EveNet: A Foundation Model for Particle Collision Data Analysis
Paper
Pretrained on 500 million simulated collision events, EveNet outperforms leading baselines across diverse analysis tasks and demonstrates transfer to experimental data using CMS Open Data. arXiv:2601.17126
